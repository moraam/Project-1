Tunapanda institute is an institute that specializes in training and clientele work by offering different services and products. All these revolves around three pillars. In the trained by pillar, we equip the youth in Nairobi and some parts of East Africa with technology, design, business and ICT-related skills through an intensive three-month training program. A portion of the graduates go into entry level jobs with companies like M-kopa. The rest join the apprenticeship program for eight months where they get to advance their desired skills while they train others based on the skills they acquired while going through the program. We fund everything in training through the second pillar, produced by.

Our apprentices and journeymen work with various clients around the world in web design, HCD research, corporate training, software and app development among others to make this possible. In powered by, we have been able to partner with an organisation in Turkana County and cloned our training model. I was part of the team that went there for the training and we successfully graduated 28 trainees . Eleven of them have been absorbed in the county government of Turkana while 4 out of the 11 will be the next trainers of the second cohort and the remaining 7 have joined start-up lions which is an incubation company.
