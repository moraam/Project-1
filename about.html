Agent Of Change

Crowing Achievements

The replication of an education model and an initiative for young women known as TechDada. TechDada is situated in Turkana, a county in Kenya that has limited access to resources and information.The education model replication was done by initiating a three-month intensive training program for the youth in areas involving technology, design, business, life skills and ICT-related skills.

The course is aimed at exposing them to the local and global market. The first cohort successfully graduated 28 trainees, the current one has 30 trainees with an increase of 21.3% females. The vision for the initiative for young women is to see young women confidently pursuing opportunities in technological fields and seeking to change perceptions in technology and promote digital inclusion for young women.

Human Centred Design Research – I was part of a team that carried out Human Centred Design research for a US-based company known as Certell. The research was aimed at developing an offline Educational Android Application for economics to be deployed in areas with limited resources and connectivity.

Skills Set Equipping – Currently, the education system is all about getting papers from recognised institutions and getting jobs but what we really lack is certain skill sets for both learning and employment. At Tunapanda we believe that if the education system was more focused on equipping learners with 21st century skills to help them maneuver their way.
